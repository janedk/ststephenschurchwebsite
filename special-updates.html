Area 2 : Teaneck, Wayne, North Haledon, Jersey City, Midland Park, Little Ferry

Area Coordinator : Leena George
We were blessed this 2018 Lenten season to come together and enjoy three Sundays of  intimate fellowship, to praise, worship and meditate on the sacred sacrifice of our Lord Jesus Christ.
Our neighbors Jimmy, Titty and Jerish opened their homes for our area.  Our Achen led the group the first week and then Titty and Jerish imparted their heart filled messages that inspired and moved us to pray in unison for all our blessings, our church and our own families. Our children led the Lenten prayers each week and sung the songs in harmony. All participants left each week wanting more of this small but powerful experience.
As it is in humility we learned to harvest good sense and wisdom. Gaining God’s perspective is not a one-time event but a lifetime of leaning on others to grow in our understanding. We hope to continue this blessed experience. Thank you to all Area II participants and host's. God Bless.
